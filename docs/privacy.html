Privacy Policy

Kapselki

Kapselki is an offline arcade game. It does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Data Collection

Kapselki does not collect personal information, contact information, location data, identifiers, gameplay analytics, diagnostics, or user-generated content.

Accounts And Sign-In

The app does not require an account, sign-in, or external service login.

Network, Ads, And Purchases

The app does not use advertising, tracking, third-party analytics, in-app purchases, or network services for gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Because Kapselki does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support link listed on the Kapselki App Store product page.
